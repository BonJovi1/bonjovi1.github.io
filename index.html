Abhinav Gupta

Master's in Computer Science at Georgia Tech

Hi there! I’m a grad student at the Georgia Institute of Technology, pursuing my Master’s in Computer Science with a specialization in Machine Learning. I work as a Graduate Teaching Assistant for CS-3630: Introduction to Perception and Robotics at the School of Interactive Computing. I love writing code to build user-friendly software and training deep neural networks to accomplish wondrous computer vision tasks!

Previously, I completed my Bachelor’s in Computer Science and Engineering from IIIT Hyderabad. I was extremely lucky to be advised by the amazing Prof. K. Madhava Krishna at the Robotics Research Center (RRC). I worked mainly on vision-guided robot navigation tasks and was fortunate to collaborate with Prof. P.J. Narayanan and Prof. Avinash Sharma during my time there. I also spent a summer at Siemens Corporate Research in Bangalore, India, where I worked with the Advanced Data Management research group.

I wish to use my knowledge of software development and machine learning to build efficient software programs that would create a meaningful impact on people’s everyday lives, whilst further pushing the boundaries of human knowledge.
